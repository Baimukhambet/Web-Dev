Borsh

  • Time: 1.5-2 hours
  • Serves: 6-8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 400-500gr
Potato 4(medium size)
Carrot 1
Tomato pasta 2(big spoons)
Cabbage 300gr
Onion 3(medium size)
Beet 2 pieces

Step 1: Put water and meat into a boil on a medium fire.

Step 2: When water have boiled. Close the bowl and keep cooking on small fire.

Step 3: Cut the vegetables and make a little fry.

Step 4: Put everything into the bowl, including beet. Delicious!
